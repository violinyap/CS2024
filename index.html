SG Money Lender welcomes everyone and we strive to create the best loan plan for all.
Regardless if you are employed or self-employed, you are eligible for a loan as long as you are 18 years old and above. We are confident that we will be able to assist you professionally in providing a suitable loan solution which specially caters to your needs.

Legal moneylenders such as SG Money Lender abide by the rules set by the Ministry of Law:
  • Licensed moneylenders are not allowed to charge a fee exceeding $60 for each month of late repayment.
  • Licensed moneylenders should not charge a fee exceeding 10% of the principal loan amount.
The maximum rate of interest they can charge is 4% per month. For late repayment, the maximum rate of late interest is 4% for each overdue month.

For Singaporeans and Permanent Residents (PRs):
  • If your annual income is less than $20,000, you can borrow up to $3,000.
  • If your annual income is $20,000 and above, you can borrow up to 6 times of your monthly income.
For foreigners:
  • If your annual income is less than $10,000, you may borrow up to $500.
  • If your annual income is at least $10,000, but less than $20,000, you may borrow up to $3,000.
  • If your annual income is at least $20,000, you can borrow up to 6 times of your monthly income.

For Singaporeans and Permanent Residents (PRs), please prepare your:
  • NRIC / Proof of identity
  • CPF / Bank statements / Income Proof
For foreigners, please prepare your:
  • Work permit / S Pass / E Pass
  • Address / Proof of Residence / Phone Bills / Utility Bills
  • Bank statements / Income proof
