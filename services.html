Personal Loans

We offer a Personal Loan with fixed monthly repayments for both Singaporeans and foreigners.

Singaporeans need a minimum annual income of $20,000 to qualify while foreigners need to be earning at least $45,000 annually. You can borrow up to 6 times of your monthly salary.

Loan repayment period ranges from 12 to 60 months with fixed and regular monthly repayments, providing flexibility to the borrowers. Repayments can be done conveniently via online platforms, mobile banking, cheque deposit or at cash deposit machines.
